For Everyone

That was one of the things that we were trying to do when I was talking about having a soda fountain where everybody could come because we literally have every type of person come here. If you go to some restaurants, it’s like you wouldn’t go in unless you were a certain type or crowd or age group or demographic or dressed a certain way or whatever. Here, we’ll have somebody where I don’t even know if they’re homeless or what, and they’re sitting right next to a guy in a 45 piece suit who’s the head of the Pulitzer Foundation or something. We just get them all. Families, kids, dates, old people, young people… Really, everybody comes.
